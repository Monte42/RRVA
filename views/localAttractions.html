    Golf

    Hanna Country Club

    hanahcountryresort.com

    Resting on a Margaretville mountain top, Hanna Mountain Resort & Country Club is a top-notch golf resort with unmatched views and service. Hanna offers a full 18 holes with creeks, boulders, and other unique features to give a challenging course in a relaxing environment.

    576 W. Hubbell Hill Road Margaretville, NY

    (800) 752-6494

    Meadows Golf Center

    meadowsgolfcenter.com

    Meadows Golf course is a 9 hole par 3 three golf course right on the side of NY 28.

    42565 NY-28 Margaretville, NY

    (845) 586-4104

    Shepard Hills Golf Club

    shephardhills.com

    Shepard Hills Golf Club is not only a beautiful and challenging course, but also has a lot of history. The stone club house was originally a guest cottage, built on the Helen Gould estate. The contruction of the 9 hole course began in 1916.

    562 Shephard Hill Road, Roxbury, NY

    (607) 326-7121

    Skiing/ Snowboarding

    Plattekill Mountain

    plattekill.com

    Found deep in the Western Catskill mountains, Plattekill is a family-owned ski resort. Due to the great location of Plattekill, it isn't uncommon to find fresh snow, even if just an inch, when you arrive for a day of skiing. Plattekill is a well know downhill mountain bike resort.

    469 Plattekill Road Roxbury, NY

    (607) 326-3500

    Bellyeare Mountain

    bellyeare.com

    Bellyeare Mountain is easy to find and a great place to ski or ride. Bellyeare has an adaptive skiing instruction program, combine that with their snowmaking, Bellyeare will always offer a great day on the mountain.

    33 Friendship Manor Road, Pine Hill NY

    (845) 254-619

    Windham Mountain

    windhammountain.com

    Windham Mountain is a double face mountain that offers all the terrain of other major mountains plus a golf course and spa. Windham dominates park riding with 6 different parks and over 80 features. Adding all the summertime activities, Windham makes a great mountain for year-round fun.

    19 Resort Drive, Windham, NY

    (518) 734-5372

    Hunter Mountain

    huntermtn.com

    The furthest mountain, Hunter Mountain offers a must have experience. Hunter has three skiable faces and multiple parks built for every level of skier/rider. Hunter is an ever-changing and a fast-growing mountain. Hunter is also home to some of the Catskill's biggest festivals, like "Taste Of Country".

    64 Klein Ave, Hunter NY

    888 486-8376

    pepacton.com

    Susans Pleasant Pheasant Farm

    kayakingnewyork.com

    In the town of Halcottsville, along the shores of Lake Wawaka, nestled next to a waterfall, you can find Susans Pleasant Pheasant Farm. With kayaks, bicycles, hiking trails, and sweeping water views, there will never be a dull moment.

    1 Bragg Hollow Road Halcottsville, NY

    (607) 326-4266

    BC's Climbing Gym

    bcclimbinggym.com

    As an indoor climbing gym, every weather is good weather for climbing. BC's Climbing Gym offers plenty of challenging courses to climb. If you need the gear, they have it all here, ready to rent out. BC's Gym also offers classes for climbing and belaying.

    234 Main Street New Paltz, NY

    (845) 255-7625

    Tinker Town Tube Rental

    towntinker.com

    Go for an exciting adventure down the Esopus Creek, with Tinker Town Tube Rental. This isn't a "lazy" river, The Esopus Creek is a class II white water experience with 1-3 foot rapids. Tinker Town has everything you need from the tubes to safety equipment.

    10 Bridge Street Phoenicia, NY

    (845) 688-5535

    Delaware and Ulster Railroad

    durr.org

    Take a ride down one of our oldest features of our area, the Delaware and Ulster Railroad offer a train ride on vintage passenger train carts, both opened and closed. The train goes from Arkville to Roxbury, passing through Halcottsville, on new tracks of course.

    43510 NY-28 Arkville NY

    (845) 586-3877

    catskillrecreationcenter.org

    The Catskill Recreational Center, or CRC, is fitness and recreational center with a year-round indoor pool. The CRC promotes healthy lifestyles and supports its members and guest in reaching their fitness goals.

    651 County Highway 38 Arkville, NY

    (845) 586-6250

    blueskyfarmwinery.com
