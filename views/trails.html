    As you can here, the Roxbury Run Village has its own selection of trails to choose from. With trails on both the north and south side of CR8 you'll have room to explore and enjoy nature in all its splendor. In the Spring, summer, and fall the trails are cut and maintained, and with a pair of snowshoes the trails can be enjoyed year round.
