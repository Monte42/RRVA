    APPROVED WINDOWS, SLIDING DOORS AND SKYLIGHTS

    Permits are required for all of the above replacements and or additions. See below for the requirements:

    Before any work is started on any of the above, you must first submit an “Application to Modify Exterior” form to the Association, with the number of windows, sliding doors and/or skylights that are being replaced and their location. Barring any unusual request, a permit will be issued. If you wish to install any other brand and/or type you are required to first apply to the Association with all the specifics, including a product brochure.

    WINDOWS and Doors - before ordering any window or door, your contractor is required to speak with Richard Andersen, Maintenance Manager.

    Your contractor is required to come to this office and meet with Richard Andersen to see an example of the window’s requirements for exterior trim, etc. Your contractor is required to supply measurements to the distributor.

    1) If there is a bank of windows (2 or more next to each other) all must be replaced. The brand you chose will be used for future replacements.
    2) They must be the same size and style (casement) as the windows you are replacing and/or previously installed.
    Windows for Clusters 1,2,3,4,32,33 are:
    1) Simonton Windows: Prime- “Reflections - 550 Series” Vinyl – White: available at Bradco Supply Corp. 800-627-2320, Wadler Brothers 845-245-5500 – or a dealership of your choice.
    2) Andersen Windows - Vinyl Clad – White ~ Not the exact same size and installation will be more labor intensive.
    3) Pella Windows – 350 Series – WHITE

    Windows for Clusters 5,6,8,9,44,45 are:

    1) Andersen Windows - Vinyl clad in Terratone. Not exact same size. Installation more labor intensive
    2) Jeld-Wen Windows – The Caradco Collection ~ Aluminum clad in Brown. This is what is currently installed. Available at Home Depot
    3) Pella Windows – 350 Series - BROWN

    SLIDING DOORS – Must be same style and size as the ones you are replacing
    Clusters 1,2,3,4,32,and 33 must be white doors
    Clusters 5,6,8,9, 44 and 45 must be brown doors- Andersen goes by the color “terratone”
    Andersen Gliding Patio Doors (Vinyl Clad) Andersen French Wood Door (Ext Vinyl Clad)
    PS 5 - R or L FWG 5068 R or L
    PS 8 - R or L FWG 8068 R or L
    Pella Pro Line Sliding Doors (Exterior Aluminum Clad) Simington Sliding Door (All Vinyl ) No Brown
    5' R or L 5’ R or L
    8’ R or L 8’ R or L
    SKYLIGHTS : If there is more than one skylight they must all be replaced.
    Replacements: Velux FS/VS C08 or FS/VS C06 depending upon what size you currently have.
    New: Same models as above - submit a drawing of the roof, showing approximate location, with measurements in relationship to top bottom and both sides of roof, with the size skylight, fixed or vented.
